Game Modification: RPS Fusion(Rock Paper Scissors)
RPS Fusion is a co-op hand game in which two teams of two duke it out. If players fuse correctly, they can create powerful swords, mighty walls, and dangerous books to destroy their opponent. If they fail, their tools will break down into pebbles, scrap metal, and scrap paper, and they are at a disadvantage. To fuse or not to fuse? That is the question! .

(Final) Digital Game: Flumen Apocalypta
A Fallout new Vegas inspired RPG created in twine. This game takes the player up and down the flooded post apocalyptic Mississippi river, encountering landmarks, factions, and river monsters. Features include an Inventory and saving system, ship upgrades, Randomized ship travel encounters, and trans characters.
